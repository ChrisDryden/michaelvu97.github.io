Magic DPM Analysis

EliseIAintBronze

Objective: Compare changes in magic damage dealt in patch 5.11 and the AP item changes in patch 5.14.
This project analyzes a set of unranked NA 5v5 games before and after the AP itemization change, and creates a histogram of Magic DPM from both before and after. The purpose is to analyze the change in the overall magic DPM tendency when the AP items were changed.
